How to get pre-qualified in 5 easy steps.

The BuildTwin pre-qualification program hinges on the collective technical proficiency of your team. Our assessment
measures the depth and breadth of your company’s capabilities to ensure they meet our marketplace's rigorous standards:
  • Technical Capabilities
  • Team
  • Commercial & Insurance
  • Work with AI Design Manager
  • AI based quality management
  • Business Listing

Technical Capabilities

Team Expertise: We evaluate the aggregate qualifications and certifications of your personnel, emphasizing the strength of your team in delivering technical solutions.

Portfolio Review: A thorough analysis of your company’s previous projects assesses your capacity to handle a range of technical challenges and deliver innovative solutions effectively.

Team

We assess the communication skills, leadership abilities, and client engagement strategies of your project managers to ensure they can facilitate seamless collaboration and maintain clear channels of communication.

Client Satisfaction Record: Past client testimonials and project outcomes are examined to understand the track record of your project managers in delivering client satisfaction.

Commercial & Insurance

The financial viability and risk management strategies of your company are of paramount importance. As part of our pre-qualification process, we conduct an review to ensure that your company is commercially robust.

Liability Insurance Verification: We check for comprehensive liability insurance coverage that aligns with the scope of the services you offer. This is to ensure that both your company and our clients are protected against potential risks.

Work with AI Design Manager

To meet our pre-qualification standards, your team must complete at least
two live projects with the AI Design Manager.

This requirement ensures that you are well-versed in contemporary AI-assisted project management techniques and prepared to deliver the highest standards of service to clients on the BuildTwin Marketplace.

Engage with the AI Design Manager to not only streamline your design process but also yields you significant efficiency gains and a competitive advantage.

AI Based Quality Management

A core aspect of the BuildTwin pre-qualification program is ensuring that your team maintain the highest quality standards. This is where our AI Quality Manager comes into play, a sophisticated tool designed to work alongside your team to oversee and enhance the quality management of your projects

Finalizing Your Business Profile Listing

Upon successful completion of the pre-qualification process, your service offering will be showcased with a comprehensive business profile listing on the BuildTwin Marketplace. This profile is not just a listing; it is a testament to your company's dedication and capability, crafted to enhance your visibility and appeal to potential clients.
