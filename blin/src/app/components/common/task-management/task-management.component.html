Digital Procurement

Procuring competent technical teams for complex projects made easy.

Tendering

BuildTwin assists you in tendering your project with an easy 3-step process. You can tender in private, only invite selected vendors, or tender to the open marketplace with fine granularity in data privacy settings.

Bidding & Matching

BuildTwin's sophisticated AI system finds the right vendors and enables an easy-to-run bidding process with sampling, verifying information, and running a pre-qualification process.

Awarding

BuildTwin creates an easy-to-review and compare bidding results, allowing you to dive deeper into the different bids and their nuances.

Commercial contracting

You sign a contract directly with the vendor, while BuildTwin supports a fully digital closing process that meets your compliance standards.
