A new generation of Quality Management.

BuildTwin comes with a built-in quality management system. The AI system learns what needs to be checked and custom generates checkpoints and checklists.

  • AI generated CheckList and Quality Gates

    BuildTwin generates Checklists based on the user, deliverables and the discussed technical decision. The checklist also considers RFIs, Revisions and many other factors.

  • Continous learning

    The digital self management portal provides custom feedback, trainings and benchmarks based on quality issues, technical queries and general learning materials.

  • Documenting everything

    BuildTwin tracks and documents all activitis across all channels - and presents a easy to use and understand cockpit.
