Course Description

Embark on a transformative journey with our BuildTwin program, meticulously designed for engineers aspiring to excel in the German market. Over a comprehensive 100-day curriculum, this course offers an unparalleled deep dive into the essentials of German engineering practices, equipping you with the skills to tackle real-world projects with confidence.

Key Highlights:
Foundation of Planning: Gain in-depth insights into the strategic foundation necessary for thriving in the German engineering landscape, focusing on efficient and safe planning methodologies.

ALLPLAN Mastery: Learn to navigate and exploit the full potential of ALLPLAN, the leading-edge software in engineering design and project management, to craft and implement smart technical solutions.

Site Management Excellence: Discover the intricacies of managing precast yards and construction sites, ensuring seamless operations and impeccable planning to meet stringent requirements.

Hands-On Learning: Through a blend of theoretical knowledge and practical application, this course emphasizes hands-on experiences, preparing you to apply your skills immediately in professional settings.

Designed by industry experts, including seasoned engineers with extensive experience in Germany's structural and civil engineering sectors, the BuildTwin program is your gateway to becoming a proficient engineering professional capable of meeting the challenges of the German market head-on.

Who this course is for

This course is specifically tailored for individuals keen on understanding the intricacies of the German market and aspiring to work according to its high standards. Whether you're a budding engineer looking to make your mark in the German engineering sector or a seasoned professional aiming to align your skills with the rigorous demands of the German market, this program offers the knowledge and practical experience you need.
