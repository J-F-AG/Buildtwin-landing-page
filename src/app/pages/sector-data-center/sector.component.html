Data Centers Tiers
  • Tier 1 | Basic Site Infrastructure: Ideal for smaller businesses, offering a basic level of uptime and cost-effective operational solutions.
  • Tier 2 | Redundant Components: Provides extra safeguards with additional power and cooling resources, suitable for medium-level operational demands.
  • Tier 3 | Concurrently Maintainable: Designed to allow any component maintenance without affecting the data center’s operational continuity.
  • Tier 4 | Fault Tolerant: Ensures the highest operational continuity with complete redundancy in all critical components and systems.
