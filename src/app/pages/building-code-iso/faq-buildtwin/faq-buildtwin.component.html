Common questions about ISO standards and its services

What does ISO stand for, and why is it important in construction?

ISO stands for the International Organization for Standardization. Its criteria guarantee safety, quality, and sustainability which are of paramount importance for trustworthy building practices.

Which ISO standards are most important for the construction industry?

Main (International Organisation for Standardization) standards are ISO 19650, ISO 9001 (Quality Management), ISO 14001 (Environmental Management) and ISO 45001 (Occupational Health and Safety).

How does ISO certification benefit construction projects?

ISO certification leads to increased efficiency, improved safety, and compliance, resulting in a successful project completion.
