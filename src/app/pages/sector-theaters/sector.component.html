Expertise Across Diverse Theater Types Globally

Our vendors provide tailored solutions for all types of theaters so you can create captivating performance spaces exceeding norms

Proscenium Theatres

Classic venues featuring a fixed stage with a prominent proscenium arch, banked seating, and a dedicated auditorium. Designed for optimal acoustics and clear sightlines, these theaters seamlessly blend traditional aesthetics with modern engineering.

Thrust Stage Theatres

Innovative performance spaces where the stage extends into the audience, creating an intimate environment, direct engagement between actors and spectators. Ideal for dynamic theatrical productions, live music, and contemporary dance.

Arena & In-the-Round Theatres

Versatile auditoria with seating arranged around a central stage for 360° viewing. Engineered for multi-angle performance, perfect for large-scale events, concerts, and community productions while maintaining superior acoustics and safety.

Black-Box/Studio Theatres

Flexible, minimalist spaces offering reconfigurable seating and stage layouts. Ideal for experimental drama, rehearsals, and innovative artistic expressions, these venues empower designers to reimagine performance spaces.
