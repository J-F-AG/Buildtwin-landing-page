Good to know...

Pricing and capabilities can be complex, so we've made it simple. If you still have questions, you can contact our team.

How are payment fees calculated?

BuildTwin charges a per-transaction fee depending on the payment method used by the end customer. Our transparent pricing ensures you only pay for successful transactions with no hidden fees.

Will I be charged tax?

BuildTwin is required to charge tax on certain products and services. Tax is charged based on your location and/or your business/tax status, which determines whether a product or service is subject to taxes, and the applicable tax rates.

Are there additional fees for refunds?

For businesses on standard pricing: There may be fees for refunds when bank transfers are used. For all other payment methods, there are no fees for issuing refunds. The payment processing fees from the original transaction are not returned.

Do you have setup fees or monthly fees?

BuildTwin does not charge setup fees, monthly fees, or any other hidden fees like closure fees. All fees for businesses on standard pricing are listed on our website.

Do you offer any discounts?

BuildTwin offers custom pricing for businesses with large processing volumes. Eligibility varies by market. Contact our sales team to discuss volume-based pricing options.

What AI capabilities are included?

All BuildTwin plans include access to our AI-powered quality control, automated drawing generation, and intelligent design management tools. Higher tiers offer advanced features and faster processing.

How quickly can I get started?

Most clients can start using BuildTwin within 24 hours of signing up. Our onboarding team provides setup assistance and training to ensure smooth integration with your existing workflows.

Can I integrate with my existing tools?

Absolutely! BuildTwin integrates seamlessly with all major CAD software, project management tools, and workflow systems including AutoCAD, Revit, Tekla, and more. Setup takes less than 5 minutes.

What support do you provide?

We offer 24/7 technical support, comprehensive documentation, live chat assistance, and dedicated account management for enterprise clients. Our global team ensures support across all time zones.
