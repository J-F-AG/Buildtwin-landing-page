Good to know...

How BuildTwin works can be complex, so we've made it simple. If you still have questions, you can contact our team.

What does pre-qualified mean?

BuildTwin marketplace offers different filters to select as per your pre-qualification standard. Together with our third-party auditor we vet all documents and verify that the submitted documents are legit. As a client, you have the option to filter for solution providers based on their formal, technical, commercial, liability, and professional eligibility. For Enterprise clients BuildTwin offers tailored tendering services to clear specific pre-qualification requirements.

Who is the contractual partner for a project?

BuildTwin acts as a digital broker between the parties. The commercial contract is solely between the service provider and client. Clients can opt to use the BuildTwin standard contract or upload their own contract to be signed by service providers.

How does the commercial agreement work?

After the digital tendering process, clients have the option to negotiate, discuss, and award the solution provider for the project. BuildTwin provides purchase order export for most commercial systems on the market, ensuring seamless integration with your existing business processes.

What happens in case of a dispute?

BuildTwin carefully vets solution providers and offers an extensive rating system to learn more about them and their past performance. In cases of disputes, signed contractual terms are to be followed. However, BuildTwin offers a range of services to enable successful project delivery and conflict resolution.

How does BuildTwin ensure quality and compliance?

BuildTwin maintains strict quality standards through our comprehensive vetting process, third-party audits, and continuous monitoring of vendor performance. All solution providers must meet international compliance standards and demonstrate proven track records in their respective fields.

What types of projects does BuildTwin support?

BuildTwin supports a wide range of AEC projects including structural engineering, BIM services, CAD drafting, steel detailing, and specialized construction services. Our platform connects you with experts across all major building sectors and project types.

How quickly can I get started with BuildTwin?

Getting started with BuildTwin is quick and easy. You can create an account in minutes, browse available vendors, and start posting projects immediately. Our team provides onboarding support to ensure you maximize the platform's benefits from day one.

What support do you provide throughout the project?

BuildTwin provides comprehensive support throughout your project lifecycle, from initial vendor selection to final delivery. Our team assists with project management, quality assurance, and ensures smooth communication between all parties involved.
